Action Impact Simulator

Select the actions you are willing to take for Climate Action. Each action earns impact points. See how your choices add up!

🌱 Impact Levels: Low Impact (1-7 pts) | ⚡ Medium Impact (8-14 pts) | 🌍 High Impact (15+ pts)

Use Public Transport

Swap one car journey per week for public transport such as buses or trains to reduce your personal carbon emissions.

Impact: 4 pts

Conserve Energy

Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use. Switch to LED bulbs to lower your household energy consumption.

Impact: 3 pts

Plant Trees

Participate in local tree-planting events or plant a native tree in your garden. Trees absorb CO₂ and provide biodiversity benefits.

Impact: 5 pts

Reduce & Recycle

Cut down on single-use plastic and recycle household waste properly. Choose products with minimal packaging wherever possible.

Impact: 3 pts

Eat More Plant-Based

Reducing meat consumption, especially red meat, significantly lowers your carbon footprint. Try at least two plant-based meals per week.

Impact: 5 pts

Spread Awareness

Share reliable climate information on social media, talk to friends, or join a student campaign to inspire others to take action.

Impact: 2 pts
