Green Ambassador Programme

Become a certified sustainability leader on your university campus. This programme trains students to run awareness campaigns, organise clean-up events, and advocate for greener policies within their institution. Ambassadors receive mentorship from sustainability professionals and build a portfolio of real-world impact.

Carbon Literacy Training

Gain internationally recognised certification in climate knowledge through this one-day accredited course. You'll learn the science behind carbon emissions, understand how everyday choices contribute to your carbon footprint, and develop a personal action plan to reduce your impact.

Climate Fresk Workshop

Join this interactive three-hour workshop that makes climate science accessible and engaging. Based on IPCC reports, participants work in small teams to connect cause-and-effect relationships of climate change using illustrated cards.

Sustainability Internship Programme

Apply for paid summer placements with leading environmental organisations, green startups, and corporate sustainability teams. Interns gain hands-on experience in renewable energy policy, circular economy innovation, and carbon accounting.

Zero-Waste Campus Initiative

Join this student-led movement working to eliminate single-use plastics and food waste across university facilities. Volunteers help coordinate recycling stations, run awareness campaigns, and collaborate with catering services.
